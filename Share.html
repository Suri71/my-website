What are the small things that can make you smile even on your worst day?

It is a sunny, scorching day. I feel the humidity along with a sense of responsibility. Yes! I am at work.

I can see a couple of earth movers burying the open holes and I can clearly relate it to what I have done to my dreams.

All my dreams and desires are buried just like them- The holes. Few of my dreams are dead, few suffocating and the remaining are alive and thriving their way hard to reach the summit and feel the Sun.

All these instances or frames clearly depicts that, I'm in place where I don't belong to.

Despite the enigma, there is some sort of avidity and mirth at the bottom of my heart. It's her. Yes!! She's the one. Envisaging her smile puts a cryptic grin on my face, like 32 white pebbles embellishing a dark background. For a moment, leaving the whole crowd around in a state of perplexity, and incognizant to what's happening.

Beyond all the imperfections in my life, thinking about her pushes me harder than any.

Even if I put my life on line and make it chaos free, she is that one last piece of puzzle which inevitably holds the key to completeness of a beautiful picture making it seamless!

She fricken makes me smile!
